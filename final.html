Final Project

My name is Guillermo Hernandez and this is my final project for CSC 106.
The project is meant to recreate Galaga in a very stylistic way. The Star Wars look is fresh, clean, and easy to play.
The controls are simple, the up arrow is to move up, the left arrow to move left, the right arrow to move right, and the
bottom arrow to move down. You use the shift key to shoot the bullets, and click on the Press Button to Start the Game.
When you die, you press restart and do the same process to start again.
